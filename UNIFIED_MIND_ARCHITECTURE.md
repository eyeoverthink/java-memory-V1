## SFA (Slow Feature Analysis)

### What is SFA?

**Slow Feature Analysis** is a principle from neuroscience:

> "The brain learns by identifying features that change slowly over time."

**Example:**
- **Fast-changing:** Pixel values in a video
- **Slow-changing:** Object identity (a car remains a car across frames)

The brain learns to recognize **objects** (slow features) rather than **pixels** (fast features).
